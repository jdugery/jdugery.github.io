Featured

Sugar Rush Stripey

A striking baccatum with peach pods covered in bold red striping, giving them a bright candy like appearance. Sweet, fruity flavor with moderate heat and very productive plants.

Featured

Scotch Bonnet

Classic fruity chinense flavor originating in the Caribbean, with similar burn to a habanero. Typically ranging from 100–350k SHU.

Featured

Ghost Pepper

Also known as bhut jolokia, this cultivar once claimed the throne as the world’s hottest pepper, reaching over 1M SHU.

Featured

Rocoto

A thick-walled Peruvian pepper native to the Andes. Rarely found elsewhere. Moderate heat, around 30–100k SHU.
