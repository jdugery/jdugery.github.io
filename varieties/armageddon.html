Capsicum chinense

Armageddon

A fiery superhot originating out of the UK.

Overview

Armageddon is a blisteringly hot superhot pepper bred in the United Kingdom in 2019 by Salvatore Genovese. This F1 hybrid produces heavily wrinkled, gnarly pods with extreme heat and a sharp, fruity flavor. Because it is an F1 hybrid, seeds will not grow true to type, but they will reliably produce fiery and interesting offspring.
