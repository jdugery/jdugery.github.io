Capsicum annuum

Rezha Macedonian (Vezeni Piperka)

An old Macedonian heirloom pepper known for its dramatic corking, rich flavor, and excellent use in roasting, grilling, and drying.

Info

Rezha Macedonian, also known as Vezeni Piperka (embroidered pepper), is a distinctive Balkan heirloom pepper prized for the heavy corking and etched texture that develops across the skin as the pods mature. The name is often associated with an “engraved” or “embroidered” look, which gives the fruits a striking appearance both on the plant and after harvest.

The pods ripen from green to deep red and are valued for their rich, slightly sweet, earthy flavor with mild to medium heat. They are especially well suited for roasting, grilling, drying in strings, and grinding into flavorful pepper powder or flakes, making them both ornamental and highly useful in the kitchen.
